Our Journey

The foundation of Risers began when the Founder crossed paths with Sneaky during a casual session of Blox Fruits on Roblox. Their friendship quickly grew, and Sneaky introduced the Founder to five key individuals: Venom, Meetu, Piyush, Minato, and later, Dark Yoddha, who joined after the community had officially formed.

Over time, the group transitioned to Minecraft, specifically the Apple MC server. However, internal tensions soon arose among the members. Recognizing the importance of unity, the Founder created a peaceful Discord group — one of the first to join was Piyush. The group became a neutral ground, focused on friendship, collaboration, and rebuilding bonds.

That initiative evolved into what we now proudly call Risers. From humble beginnings, the group has grown into a full-fledged community with its own Discord server, dedicated SMP, and now, this website — a testament to our journey, resilience, and shared passion for gaming.
